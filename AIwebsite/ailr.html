Let's get our basics straight!
ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE DATA SCIENCE
MEANING: AI is basically a simulation of human intellect in machines, where they are programmed to think and act like humans, example:- visual perception, speech recognition, the ability to rationalize and make decisions and much more. MEANING: Data science is a field in tech that uses scientific methods,processes, algorithms and systems to extract information and insights from data. It then proceeds to organize and analyze the data to make it useful for various stakeholders to draw conclusions from and take decisions accordingly.
Types of AI:
There are three broad categories of AI :
  • Artificial Narrow Intelligence: Focuses on one task with limited abilities. eg. Siri
  • Artificial General Intelligence: This is an AI that may think on the same level as a human.eg. a lifelike robot.
  • Artificial Super Intelligence: This is an AI that may match up to and then surpass a human, to the point where it can have emotions and sustain relationships. eg. a super advanced robot.
The first one is a reality being explored,but the other two are yet to be understood and comprehended fully, as we still do not know enough about humans to create something superhuman artificially. 		Types of data scientists:
