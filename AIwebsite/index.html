Say hi to Pingu the penguin!
Pingu is a very simplified game bot, that plays ping pong with you.

We said he is a bot, so what do we mean by that???

To explain it simply, Pingu has been coded in such a way that he follows a simple fixed logic to follow the movements of the ball.

As he is a product of code created using the principles of math, he obviously won't get tired and won't feel any emotions while playing.

Since this is not a human attribute, we call it 'artificial'.

And since it able to follow a sequence of events and interpret things, it is safe to say Pingu is somewhat 'intelligent'.


Thus we can say that Pingu is a simple product of Artificial Intelligence. You may have thought of
AI as fancy robots walking around in sci-fi movies, but the truth is that AI is super accessible and
infact, you can make a few AI applications of your own! But for now, lets see why AI is so hyped up!

Hi, we hope you enjoyed that game. If you haven't played it yet, have a look and come back here!

Now imagine that Pingu was also the scorekeeper (don't worry, he doesn't have emotions so he won't cheat!).

Along with keeping your scores, if he also kept track of when you play during the day and for how long in a simple virtual storage location, this would be called data storage.

If Pingu gave us, the website makers, all of this data, we could find out a lot about you! For example, we could find out when your coordination is at its finest simply from the times of the day when you score the highest, we could find out how engaging the game is for you, by the number of times you play it and a lot more!
This would be called data analysis, meaning gathering useful information from the data!

Now if we used these insights to improve the game for you in ways like eg. making the game a little more challenging for you during your peak coordination hours, change features according to your playing style etc, this would be called making 'use of actionable insights'. ( in other words, act upon data analysis).

Together all of the above, constitute data science!
